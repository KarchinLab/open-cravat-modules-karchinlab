# Network of Cancer Genes

NCG is a manually curated collection of cancer genes, healthy drivers and their properties. The repository consists of 3344 well-known or predicted drivers of cancer. These genes were collected from 310 publications, including two sources of known cancer genes and 273 cancer sequencing screens of more than 100 cancer types from 34,905 cancer donors and multiple primary sites. 

This annotator includes information about cancer type, primary site, and associated organ systems for cancer associated with the given gene.
